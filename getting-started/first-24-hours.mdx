## What to Do After Installation

You've installed Looksy—now what? This guide walks you through the first 24 hours to ensure you launch successfully and start seeing results immediately.

<Note>
Virtual try-on works automatically once installed. This guide helps you test, customize, and promote the feature for maximum impact.
</Note>

## Hour 1: Test & Verify

**Goal:** Confirm virtual try-on is working correctly.

### Desktop Testing

1. **Visit a product page** on your store
2. **Look for the "Try On" button** on the product image (appears as a corner badge)
3. **Click the button** to open the try-on modal
4. **Upload a test selfie** (or use your phone's camera)
5. **Wait 15-20 seconds** for the result
6. **Verify the try-on looks realistic**

<Check>
If the button appears and try-on works, you're all set on desktop.
</Check>

### Mobile Testing

1. **Open your store on your phone** (actual device, not browser resize)
2. **Navigate to a product page**
3. **Tap "Try On"** on the product image
4. **Take a selfie or upload from photo library**
5. **Verify the result displays correctly**

<Check>
Mobile accounts for 70%+ of try-ons. Mobile testing is critical.
</Check>

### Troubleshooting

If the button doesn't appear:
- **Check app embed is enabled:** Go to Online Store → Themes → Customize → App embeds → Looksy Try-On Button → Toggle ON
- **Clear browser cache** and test in incognito mode
- **Verify you're on a product page** (not collection or homepage)

See [Common Issues](/troubleshooting/common-issues) for detailed troubleshooting.

## Hours 2-4: Customize & Configure

**Goal:** Make the feature match your brand and optimize placement.

### Customize Button Appearance

Go to **Looksy Dashboard → Customize Button** to adjust:

**Button Text Options:**
- "Try On" (default, highest engagement)
- "See it on you" (benefit-focused)
- "Virtual try-on" (descriptive)
- Custom text (your choice)

**Button Position:**
- **Top Left** (default, most visible)
- **Top Right** (alternative placement)

**Button Colors:**
- Use theme styling (automatic, recommended)
- Or customize colors to match your brand

<Tip>
Start with defaults. They're optimized for engagement. Customize only if needed for brand consistency.
</Tip>

### Review Product Selection

By default, **all products are enabled** for virtual try-on. You may want to disable certain products:

**Products to consider disabling:**
- Very revealing garments (lingerie, swimwear)
- Products without clear product images
- Items that don't work well (accessories, pants - if results aren't good)

**To disable products:**
1. Go to **Looksy Dashboard → Products**
2. Browse or search for products
3. Toggle off specific products
4. Click **Save**

<Note>
Start with all products enabled. Disable only if you see poor results or high error rates for specific items.
</Note>

### Check Analytics Setup

1. Go to **Looksy Dashboard → Analytics**
2. Verify data is tracking (you should see your test try-ons)
3. Note your current credit balance (starts at 25 credits on free plan)

## Hours 4-8: Plan Promotion

**Goal:** Let customers know virtual try-on is available.

### Draft Homepage Banner

Create a banner to announce the feature:

**Headline options:**
- "Try before you buy with virtual try-on"
- "See how it looks on you"
- "New: Virtual try-on available"

**Call-to-action:**
- "Shop now" → Links to collection page
- "Try it" → Links to featured product

**Banner placement:**
- Top of homepage (high visibility)
- Above product grid
- In hero section

### Plan Email Announcement

**Subject line ideas:**
- "New feature: Try on before you buy"
- "See yourself in our products"
- "Virtual try-on is now available"

**Email content:**
1. Announce the feature
2. Explain how it works (simple 3-step process)
3. Highlight benefits (confidence, fewer returns)
4. Include product images with "Try On" button visible
5. Call-to-action to shop and try

**Timing:** Send within 24-48 hours of launch

### Schedule Social Media Posts

**Platform-specific content:**

**Instagram:**
- Post showing before/after try-on
- Story with "Swipe up to try"
- Reel demonstrating the feature

**Facebook:**
- Announcement post
- Link to featured product
- Encourage comments and shares

**TikTok:**
- Quick demo video
- Show customer using feature
- "POV: Shopping online just got easier"

**Twitter/X:**
- Short announcement
- GIF showing try-on process
- Link to store

### Add to Product Descriptions

Update product descriptions to mention virtual try-on:

**Example copy:**
> "Use our virtual try-on feature to see how this looks on you. Just click 'Try On' above the product image and upload a selfie."

Add this to:
- Top-performing products (first priority)
- New arrivals
- Products with high return rates

## Hours 8-12: Optimize Visibility

**Goal:** Ensure the button is visible and obvious to customers.

### Check Button Visibility

**On desktop:**
- Is the button visible without hovering?
- Does it stand out from the product image?
- Is it clearly labeled?

**On mobile:**
- Is it large enough to tap easily?
- Does it appear above the fold?
- Is text readable?

### Test on Multiple Browsers

Test on:
- Chrome (desktop & mobile)
- Safari (desktop & mobile)
- Firefox
- Edge

Verify button appears consistently across browsers.

### Gather Feedback

**Ask team members to test:**
- How intuitive is the button placement?
- Is the try-on process clear?
- Any confusion points?

**Make adjustments based on feedback.**

## Hours 12-24: Monitor & Iterate

**Goal:** Track initial usage and identify any issues.

### Check Analytics

Go to **Looksy Dashboard → Analytics** and review:

**Key metrics:**
- **Total try-ons:** How many customers have used it?
- **Top products:** Which products are getting tried on most?
- **Credit usage:** How fast are you using credits?

**What to expect in first 24 hours:**
- 5-20 try-ons (depending on traffic)
- 1-5 products tried on
- 5-20 credits used

<Tip>
Low usage in first 24 hours is normal. Promote the feature to increase awareness.
</Tip>

### Note Any Issues

**Common issues to watch for:**
- Button not appearing on certain products
- Try-on errors for specific products
- Mobile layout issues
- Slow loading times

**Document issues and refer to:**
- [Common Issues](/troubleshooting/common-issues)
- [Image Quality](/troubleshooting/image-quality)
- [Performance](/troubleshooting/performance)

### Monitor Credit Usage

**Credit consumption:**
- Free plan: 25 credits
- Each try-on uses 1 credit
- Monitor daily usage

**When to upgrade:**
- Running low on credits (< 10 remaining)
- High engagement (> 100 try-ons/month)
- Want advanced features

See [Pricing](/faq/pricing-and-plans) for plan options.

### Plan Next Steps

**Week 1 priorities:**
1. **Promote the feature** (homepage, email, social)
2. **Monitor performance** (engagement, credits, issues)
3. **Optimize images** (ensure high quality product photos)
4. **Gather customer feedback** (surveys, comments)

**Week 2 priorities:**
1. **Analyze data** (which products perform best?)
2. **A/B test button copy** (if engagement is low)
3. **Expand promotion** (ads, influencers, partnerships)

## Common Questions

### How many try-ons should I expect in the first 24 hours?

**Typical range:** 5-20 try-ons

**Factors that increase usage:**
- High traffic (> 500 visitors/day)
- Active promotion (homepage banner, email)
- Clear button visibility
- Mobile-optimized experience

### Should I promote the feature immediately?

**Yes.** Customers won't know virtual try-on is available unless you tell them. Promote from day 1 for maximum impact.

### What if no one uses it in the first 24 hours?

**Normal for low-traffic stores.** If you have < 100 visitors/day, it may take a few days to see usage.

**Action steps:**
- Verify button is visible
- Test on mobile
- Promote via email and social media
- Check product page traffic

### When should I upgrade to a paid plan?

**Upgrade when:**
- You've used 20+ of your 25 free credits
- You're seeing consistent daily usage
- You want more try-ons/month

See [Pricing](/faq/pricing-and-plans) for plan options.
