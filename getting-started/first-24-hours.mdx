## What should I do after installing Looksy?

Use the first day to prove the experience works on your actual theme and establish a baseline. Do not judge conversion or returns from a handful of early try-ons.

## Storefront verification

- Confirm the intended theme has the Looksy app embed enabled and saved.
- Open several targeted product pages, including a product with variants if relevant.
- Verify the button appears over the main product image, remains readable and keyboard reachable, and does not cover important details or controls.
- Complete a try-on with a clear photo of one person.
- Confirm the generated preview appears in the same product-image area.
- Repeat on representative desktop and mobile browsers from your store analytics.
- Check that the preview is presented as visualization rather than proof of size or fit.

## Merchant configuration

- Review the products and collections where try-on is enabled.
- Check exceptions and variant behavior.
- Review the product-image corner and appearance controls in the Shopify theme editor.
- Keep the default wording if a custom label would be less clear.
- Record the current plan and remaining credits before broader promotion.

## Measurement baseline

Capture a dated baseline for:

- Looksy-engaged sessions and recorded try-ons;
- product-level try-on activity;
- add-to-cart activity after try-on, when available;
- current Shopify orders, conversion rate, and returns for the chosen product set;
- customer-support questions about fit, appearance, uploads, or privacy.

Use the definitions displayed in Looksy. A metric can be delayed, partial, or unavailable depending on tracking and integrations.

## Promotion check

Tell shoppers what the feature does without overpromising. Suitable language includes "Create a visual preview" or "See the style on your photo." Avoid guarantees about fit, conversion, returns, accuracy, or speed.

## If something fails

Record the store URL, product and variant, theme name, device/browser, time, exact message, and a screenshot. Recheck the app embed and product targeting before contacting [Looksy support](mailto:caleb@withlooksy.com).
