## Prerequisites

Before you begin:

- You have a Shopify store
- You're logged into your Shopify admin
- You have at least one apparel product listed

<Note>
  Looksy works with all Shopify plans and themes. No developer required.
</Note>

## Step 1: Install Looksy

1. Visit the [Looksy app on the Shopify App Store](https://apps.shopify.com/looksy)
2. Click **"Install App"**
4. Click **"Install"** to confirm
4. You'll be redirected to your Shopify admin

<Check>
  Installation takes 30 seconds. Once complete, you'll land in the Looksy dashboard.
</Check>

## Step 2: Enable Products

Choose which products will have virtual try-on:

### Option A: Enable by Collection (Recommended)

1. In the Looksy dashboard, click **"Enable by Collection"**
2. Select the collection(s) you want to enable (e.g., "Dresses," "Tops," "New Arrivals")
3. Click **"Save"**

**Benefits:**

- All products in the collection are automatically enabled
- New products added to the collection are automatically included
- Easy to manage and scale

### Option B: Enable Individual Products

1. In the Looksy dashboard, click **"Enable by Product"**
2. Search for products and toggle them on
3. Click **"Save"**

**Benefits:**

- Fine-tuned control over which products offer try-on
- Test on bestsellers first
- Gradually roll out the feature

<Tip>
  Start with 10-20 products in categories that work well (tops, dresses, jackets). Expand based on performance.
</Tip>

## Step 3: Add the Try-On Button to Your Store

The Looksy button is added using Shopify app embeds:

1. Go to **Online Store → Themes** in your Shopify admin
2. Click **"Customize"** on your active theme
3. In the theme editor, click on the **"App embeds"** section (usually in the left sidebar)
4. Find **"Looksy Virtual Try-On"** and toggle it **ON**
5. Click **"Save"** in the top-right corner

<Check>
  The "Try On" button will now appear on all enabled product pages.
</Check>

### Customize Button Styling (Optional)

The button appears on your product images. You can customize how it looks:

1. Go to **Looksy admin → Customize Button**
2. Adjust **button text** ("Try On" is default)
3. Change **colors** to match your brand
4. Adjust **size** (padding, font size)
5. Choose **corner position** (top left or top right)
6. Click **"Save"**

## Step 4: Test It

Visit a product page on your live store and try it out:

1. Navigate to a product with virtual try-on enabled
2. Click **"Try On"**
3. Upload a selfie (or take one with your camera)
4. See the result in seconds

<Tip>
  Use a full body shot for best results. Make sure only one person is in the photo.
</Tip>

### What to Check

- Does the button appear on the product page?
- Is it easy to find and click?
- Does the try-on render quickly and accurately?
- Does it work on mobile devices?

## Step 5: Optimize for Best Results

### Product Images

Looksy works with any product images. What helps accuracy:

- **Multiple angles** – Upload different views (max 4 images per product)
- **Detail shots** – Close-ups showing texture and material
- **Any background works** – Plain, complex, or busy backgrounds all work
- **Higher resolution helps** – 1200px+ recommended but not required

[Learn more about image requirements →](/product-setup/image-requirements)

### Button Styling

Customize the button to make it stand out:

- **High contrast colors** – Choose colors that pop against product images
- **Clear button text** – "Try On" is short and action-oriented
- **Appropriate size** – Not too small, not too large
- **Corner position** – Top left or top right based on your product images
- **Clear, action-oriented copy** (e.g., "Try On")

### Mobile Testing

Test the experience on your phone:

- Is the button easy to tap?
- Does the upload flow work smoothly?
- Do results render quickly?

## Step 6: Promote the Feature

Let customers know virtual try-on is available:

### On Your Store

- Add a banner to your homepage: "Try clothes on virtually before you buy"
- Mention it on collection pages
- Include in product descriptions: "Use our virtual try-on to see it on yourself"

### In Marketing

- Feature in new product launch emails
- Promote on social media
- Highlight in abandoned cart emails
- Create tutorial content showing how to use it

## Step 7: Track Performance

Monitor results in your Looksy dashboard:

- **Engagement rate** – What % of visitors use virtual try-on?
- **Conversion lift** – Do products with try-on convert better?
- **Return rate** – Are returns lower for try-on products?
- **Product performance** – Which products drive the most engagement?

[Learn more about analytics →](/analytics/dashboard-overview)

## Common Setup Issues

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="The button doesn't appear on my product pages">
    **Solution:** Ensure you've enabled the Looksy app embed in your theme settings (Online Store → Themes → Customize → App embeds). Also verify that the product is enabled in your Looksy dashboard.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Virtual try-on isn't working on mobile">
    **Solution:** Clear your mobile browser cache and test again. Ensure your theme is mobile-responsive and that the app embed is enabled globally (not just on desktop).
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Results don't look accurate">
    **Solution:** Add multiple product images (max 4) showing different angles and details. The AI uses up to 4 images for better accuracy. Looksy works with any images—don't reshoot or adjust backgrounds/lighting.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Button styling looks wrong">
    **Solution:** Go to Looksy admin → Customize Button. Adjust colors, text, size, and corner position (top left or top right). The button appears on product images and cannot be moved elsewhere.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Quick Setup Checklist

Complete these steps to go live with Looksy:

- Install Looksy from the Shopify App Store
- Enable products (by collection or individually)
- Turn on the Looksy app embed in theme settings
- Test on a live product page (desktop and mobile)
- Verify image quality meets standards
- Ensure button is visible and easy to find
- Promote the feature on your homepage
- Set up conversion tracking in analytics

<Check>
  Once these steps are complete, you're live with virtual try-on!
</Check>
