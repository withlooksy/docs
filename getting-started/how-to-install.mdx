## Getting Started with Looksy

Looksy is AI virtual try-on for Shopify. Customers upload a photo and see how your products look on them. The app works out of the box with zero configuration.

## Installation (2 Minutes)

### Step 1: Install from Shopify App Store

1. Visit the [Looksy app page](https://apps.shopify.com/looksy) on the Shopify App Store
2. Click **Add app**
3. Approve the installation in your Shopify admin
4. You'll land in the Looksy dashboard

**You're automatically subscribed to the free plan (25 credits).**

### Step 2: Enable the Try-On Button

The "Try On" button displays on your product images. Enable it using Shopify's app embed:

1. During onboarding, click the button that takes you directly to app embeds
2. Find **Looksy Try-On Button** in the app embeds list
3. Toggle it on
4. Click **Save**

**That's it.** The button now appears on all product pages.

<Tip>
The button uses your theme's styling (fonts, colors) by default. You can customize it later if needed.
</Tip>

### Step 3: Test It

1. Visit any product page on your store
2. Click **Try On** (button appears on the product image)
3. Upload a selfie
4. Wait 15-20 seconds for the result

---

## How It Works

When a customer clicks "Try On":

1. They upload a photo
2. Looksy validates the image (must be one person, human, showing enough body)
3. The image and your product images (max 4) run through our image-to-image AI model
4. The processed result displays on the product page

**Image validation protects your credits.** If the image is unsuitable (cartoon, animal, multiple people, insufficient body showing), the customer gets an error explaining what's wrong.

## Managing Products

**By default, Looksy enables all products.** This works for most stores.

### Disable Specific Products

If certain products don't work well with try-on, disable them:

1. Go to **Products** in your Looksy admin
2. Find the product you want to disable
3. Toggle it off

<Warning>
**Revealing products may struggle:** Lingerie, swimwear, and very revealing garments might fail to generate results. This protects against misuse. Disable these products if you encounter issues.
</Warning>

### Select Specific Products Only

To use try-on on only certain products:

1. Go to **Products** in your Looksy admin
2. Click **Disable All**
3. Enable only the products you want

## Customizing the Button

The button uses your theme's styling by default. To customize:

1. Go to your Looksy dashboard
2. Click **Customize button**
3. Adjust settings:

**Position:**
- Top Left
- Top Right

**Text & Colors:**
- Button Text (default: "Try On")
- Text & Icon Color
- Background Color

**Advanced:**
- Icon Style (Sparkle / No Icon)
- Icon Size & Weight
- Font Size & Weight
- Font Family (uses Store Body Font by default)
- Border Radius
- Padding

Changes apply immediately to all product pages.
