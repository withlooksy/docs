## Getting Started with Looksy

Welcome to **Looksy**, the AI virtual try-on that helps shoppers see themselves in your products before they buy. Looksy increases conversion rates and reduces returns by removing guesswork from online shopping.

## Installation

Installing Looksy on your Shopify store takes just a couple of steps.

1. **Install Looksy from the Shopify App Store**\
   Visit the Looksy app page on the Shopify App Store and click **Add app**.
2. **Approve the installation**\
   You’ll be redirected to your Shopify admin to confirm and install the app.
3. **Open your Looksy dashboard**\
   Once installed, you’ll land in the Looksy dashboard where you can enable virtual try-on and manage your settings.

---

## Quick Setup

## Step 1: Choose Products

Select which products you want to enable virtual try-on for.

- **By collection** (recommended): Turn on Looksy for entire collections at once
- **By product**: Enable individual products if needed

_Looksy works best with apparel such as dresses, tops, shirts, t-shirts, and jackets. Clear product images on plain backgrounds deliver the best results._

## Step 2: Add the Looksy Button to Your Store

The Looksy try-on button is added using a Shopify app embed.

- Go to **Online Store → Themes** in your Shopify admin
- Click **Customize** on your active theme
- Open **App embeds**
- Enable **Looksy Virtual Try-On**
- Save your changes

The try-on button will now appear on all enabled product pages.\
