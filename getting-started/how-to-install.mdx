## How do I install Looksy?

Install Looksy from the Shopify App Store, enable its app embed in the theme you want to test, and complete the shopper flow on representative product pages.

## 1. Install the app

1. Open the [Looksy Shopify App Store listing](https://apps.shopify.com/looksy).
2. Select **Add app**.
3. Review the permissions and installation information in Shopify.
4. Complete the installation and open Looksy.

The current Preview plan is free and includes 25 credits. Confirm the offer shown in Looksy before choosing a paid plan.

## 2. Enable the app embed

1. Open **Online Store → Themes** in Shopify.
2. Choose the theme you want to test and open **Customize**.
3. Open **App embeds**.
4. Enable Looksy and save.

<Warning>
  Use an unpublished or development theme for initial testing when your workflow allows it. Theme compatibility should be verified before publication.
</Warning>

## 3. Test representative products

Check several products and variants rather than a single example. Verify entry-point placement, instructions, image selection, processing, result display, failure and retry states, and the return to the correct product state.

## 4. Test mobile and desktop

Use representative browsers and devices from your store analytics. Test camera permission, existing-image upload, slower connections, and responsive layouts.

## 5. Review before launch

- Product and variant behavior is correct.
- The theme layout remains usable.
- Shopper expectations are clear.
- Performance is acceptable on representative devices.
- Products that do not provide a suitable experience are excluded.
- Analytics and support processes are ready.
