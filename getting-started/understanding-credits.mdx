## What are Looksy credits?

Credits are the usage allowance included with a Looksy plan. Your Looksy account is the source of truth for your current balance, billing interval, and recorded usage.

Do not estimate the number of shopper sessions from a plan's credit count. Check the usage shown in Looksy because product behavior and billing rules can change.

## Which actions use credits?

| Successful action | Credit use |
| --- | ---: |
| Product-page photo try-on | 1 credit |
| Fitting Room outfit generation | 1 credit per generated outfit, regardless of garment count |
| Looksy Studio model-image generation | 1 credit |
| Live Video Try-On | 1 credit per 2 active seconds, rounded up |

Failed photo, Fitting Room, and Studio generations do not count as successful actions. Live Video is different: a session that never begins with the video provider uses 0 credits, but once the provider session begins it uses at least 1 credit even if the shopper disconnects immediately or no active seconds are recorded. A Studio generation and a shopper try-on are separate credit-using actions.

## How does the Preview allowance work?

Preview is not a paid Shopify subscription. It allows up to 25 successful try-ons in a rolling 30-day window. It is not a one-time balance and does not reset on a fixed calendar date.

## What happens when I need more credits?

The answer depends on the paid plan's billing interval:

- **Monthly subscription:** usage beyond the included allowance is billed through the Shopify-approved usage line at the plan's additional-credit rate.
- **Annual subscription:** Shopify does not combine the annual subscription with Looksy's usage-overage line. Looksy evaluates the included allowance over a rolling 30-day usage window; new try-ons pause after that allowance is used.

The monthly additional-credit rates are:

- Starter: $0.14 per additional credit
- Growth: $0.12 per additional credit
- Scale: $0.10 per additional credit

Review the exact subscription and usage terms in Shopify before approving a plan. For account-specific billing questions, contact support from the app.

<Warning>
  Do not assume an annual plan includes paid overage. Annual subscriptions stop at their included credits for the rolling 30-day usage window.
</Warning>

## Can I set my own usage limits?

Yes. Looksy includes a merchant-controlled billing-period safety limit and an optional daily limit for anonymous shoppers. See [Usage and Access Controls](https://withlooksy.com/docs/settings/usage-and-access-controls).

## What happens to unused credits?

Refer to the terms shown in Looksy for your subscription or ask support to confirm the rule that applies to your account.

## How should I choose a plan?

1. Review the usage shown in Looksy.
2. Compare it with the included credits in each plan.
3. Decide whether monthly overage or a support-confirmed annual cadence with a hard 30-day usage limit better matches your store.
4. Set a safety limit if you need a lower merchant-controlled ceiling.
5. Confirm the current price and Shopify approval terms before subscribing.
