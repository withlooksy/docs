## What are Looksy credits?

Credits are the usage allowance included with a Looksy plan. Your Looksy account is the source of truth for your current balance and recorded usage.

Do not estimate the number of shopper sessions from a plan's credit count. Check the usage shown in Looksy because product behavior and billing rules can change.

## How many credits does each plan include?

| Plan | Included credits | Monthly price | Additional credits |
| --- | ---: | ---: | ---: |
| **Preview** | 25 | Free | Not listed |
| **Starter** | 100 | $14.99/month | $0.14 each |
| **Growth** | 300 | $29/month | $0.12 each |
| **Scale** | 600 | $79/month | $0.10 each |

| Plan | Annual price |
| --- | ---: |
| **Starter** | $149/year |
| **Growth** | $290/year |
| **Scale** | $790/year |

<Note>
  Pricing can change. Confirm the offer shown in Looksy before subscribing. You can also check the [Looksy website](https://withlooksy.com/) and [Shopify App Store listing](https://apps.shopify.com/looksy).
</Note>

## What happens when I need more credits?

Paid plans list a rate for additional credits beyond the included allowance:

- Starter: $0.14 per additional credit
- Growth: $0.12 per additional credit
- Scale: $0.10 per additional credit

Review your usage in Looksy before changing plans. For account-specific billing questions, contact support from the app.

## What happens to unused credits?

Refer to the terms shown in Looksy for your subscription or ask support to confirm the rule that applies to your account.

## How should I choose a plan?

1. Review the usage shown in Looksy.
2. Compare it with the included credits in each plan.
3. Consider the additional-credit rate if your usage regularly exceeds the allowance.
4. Confirm the current price and billing terms before subscribing.
