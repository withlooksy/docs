## Overview

Looksy works on **all fashion products**. The AI adapts to any product type and delivers realistic results across all categories.

## Apparel ✓

All clothing items work with Looksy:

### Tops & Shirts
- T-shirts and graphic tees
- Tank tops and camisoles
- Blouses and button-up shirts
- Sweaters and hoodies
- Polo shirts
- Crop tops
- Long-sleeve and short-sleeve tops

### Dresses
- Casual dresses
- Formal and cocktail dresses
- Maxi, midi, and mini dresses
- Shirt dresses and bodycon styles
- A-line and fit-and-flare dresses

### Outerwear
- Jackets (denim, leather, bomber, varsity)
- Blazers and suit jackets
- Coats and parkas
- Hoodies and zip-ups
- Cardigans and shrugs
- Vests and gilets

### Activewear
- Sports tops and athletic shirts
- Yoga tops and running shirts
- Compression tops and base layers
- Sports bras
- Gym hoodies and jackets
- Athletic pants and leggings

### Swimwear
- One-piece swimsuits
- Bikini tops and bottoms
- Tankinis
- Rash guards
- Swim trunks and board shorts

### Bottoms
- Jeans and denim
- Pants and trousers
- Shorts
- Skirts
- Leggings and tights
- Joggers

## Accessories ✓

Looksy works on fashion accessories:

- **Jewelry** – Necklaces, bracelets, earrings, rings
- **Bags** – Handbags, clutches, backpacks, totes
- **Scarves** – Scarves, wraps, bandanas
- **Hats** – Baseball caps, beanies, sun hats
- **Belts** – All belt styles
- **Sunglasses** – All eyewear styles

## Footwear ✓

Looksy works on all shoe types:

- Sneakers and athletic shoes
- Boots (ankle, knee-high, combat)
- Heels and pumps
- Sandals and flats
- Loafers and dress shoes
- Slippers and slides

## What the AI Handles

The model delivers realistic results for:

- **All patterns** – Stripes, florals, graphics, tie-dye, abstract
- **All textures** – Knits, lace, sequins, leather, denim, velvet
- **All styles** – Structured, oversized, fitted, drapey, loose
- **All colors** – Solids, prints, gradients, color-blocking
- **All materials** – Cotton, silk, wool, synthetic, blends

<Check>
There are no product type restrictions. The AI adapts to any fashion item.
</Check>

## Revealing Products

**Only limitation:** Very revealing garments may fail to generate results.

This includes:
- Lingerie
- Very revealing swimwear
- Extremely revealing clothing

**Why:** Protection against misuse. Disable these products in your admin if you encounter issues.

## Style vs. Sizing

<Warning>
**Important:** Looksy shows customers how products **look** (style), not whether they'll **fit** (sizing). Always provide size charts and fit descriptions alongside virtual try-on.
</Warning>

Virtual try-on helps with:
- Visualizing style and appearance
- Seeing colors and patterns on yourself
- Understanding how a garment looks on different body types
- Reducing style-related returns

Virtual try-on does NOT help with:
- Determining correct size
- Predicting fit
- Solving sizing issues

## Product Images

Better product images produce better try-on results:

### What Helps
- **Multiple angles** – Different views (max 4 images per product)
- **Detail shots** – Close-ups of texture and material
- **Any background** – Plain, complex, or busy all work
- **Any angle** – Front, side, angled views all work
- **Higher resolution helps** – But not required

### The AI Works With
- Any image quality (any images work)
- Any backgrounds (no background requirements)
- Any angles (no angle requirements)
- Model shots or flat lays (both work equally well)

<Tip>
There are no strict requirements. The app works with any product images, but better images produce better results.
</Tip>

## Testing Products

After enabling try-on:

1. Visit product pages on your live store
2. Click **"Try On"** with your own photo
3. Verify results look realistic
4. Check button placement

You're testing user experience, not whether the AI works—it works on all products.
