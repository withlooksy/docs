## Looksy Works With Any Product Images

Looksy works with your existing Shopify product images—no special photography or setup required. The AI adapts to any image style, background, lighting, or angle.

<Check>
You don't need to reshoot products or adjust your photography. Looksy uses whatever images you already have on Shopify.
</Check>

## What Actually Helps

While Looksy works with any images, these factors improve accuracy and realism:

### Multiple Angles (Up to 4 Images)

**What helps:**
- Different views of the garment (front, side, back, detail shots)
- Shows the AI more context about the product
- Looksy uses up to 4 product images per try-on

**Why it helps:** More angles give the AI better understanding of garment structure, fit, and details.

### Close-Ups on Texture and Material

**What helps:**
- Detail shots showing fabric texture
- Close-ups of patterns, embellishments, or prints
- Images highlighting material properties

**Why it helps:** The AI renders textures more accurately when it can see fabric details clearly.

### Showcasing Garment Details

**What helps:**
- Images that show unique design elements
- Clear views of collars, sleeves, hemlines, and other features
- Photos highlighting special details or embellishments

**Why it helps:** Better visibility of details leads to more realistic rendering in the try-on result.

## Image Quality Factors

These technical factors can improve results but are NOT required:

### Resolution

- **Higher resolution helps** – More detail for the AI to work with
- **Minimum:** 800px wide
- **Recommended:** 1200px+ wide
- **Reality:** Looksy works with any resolution; higher is better but not essential

### File Format

- **JPG or PNG** – Standard Shopify formats work perfectly
- **File size** – Any size works; Looksy uses what's on Shopify

<Note>
Looksy pulls images directly from Shopify. You don't need to worry about file formats or compression—the app handles it automatically.
</Note>

## What Doesn't Matter

These factors do NOT affect virtual try-on quality:

### Backgrounds

- ✓ **Any background works** – Plain, complex, busy, outdoor, indoor, patterned
- ✓ **No background removal needed** – The AI handles isolation automatically
- ✓ **Keep your existing photos** – No need to reshoot on white or gray backgrounds

### Lighting

- ✓ **Lighting is not critical** – The AI adapts to any lighting conditions
- ✓ **Works with natural or artificial light** – Studio lighting not required
- ✓ **Shadows and uneven lighting are fine** – The AI compensates automatically

### Angles and Framing

- ✓ **Any angle works** – Front-facing, side view, angled shots all work
- ✓ **Composition is flexible** – Centered, off-center, cropped images all work
- ✓ **Model poses don't matter** – Stylized poses, arms up/down, seated, standing all work

<Check>
The AI model is highly realistic across all product types and handles any photography style automatically.
</Check>

## Flat Lay vs. Model Photography

Both work equally well with Looksy:

### Flat Lay Photography

**Works great for:**
- T-shirts, tops, simple apparel
- Budget-friendly product photos
- Consistent product catalog

### Model Photography

**Works great for:**
- Dresses, structured garments
- Showing fit and drape
- Lifestyle product presentation

**No preference:** The AI delivers realistic results with either style.

## What You Can Do (Optional)

If you want to maximize accuracy, consider adding:

1. **Multiple product images** – Upload up to 4 images showing different angles
2. **Detail shots** – Include close-ups of fabric texture or unique design elements
3. **Higher resolution** – Use 1200px+ width if available (but not required)

<Note>
These are optional optimizations, not requirements. Looksy works out of the box with whatever images you already have.
</Note>

## Technical Specifications

### Supported Formats
- JPG, PNG (standard Shopify formats)

### Image Dimensions
- **Minimum:** 800px wide
- **Recommended:** 1200px+ wide
- **Maximum:** No limit (Shopify's limits apply)

### Images Per Product
- **Used by AI:** Up to 4 images per product
- **Pulls from:** Your Shopify product images automatically
