## What do Looksy's product defaults change?

Product defaults apply only to products added in future. Existing products keep their current targeting and imagery until you edit them.

Under **Looksy → Settings → Products**, you can:

- turn try-on on automatically for new products;
- use each new product's Shopify featured image; or
- turn off the featured-image default so Looksy can combine up to four gallery images.

Review new products after they appear in Looksy. A default does not prove that every image, appearance, variant, or product template is ready for shoppers.

## How do I change the admin language?

Under **Looksy → Settings → Language**, choose the language for the Looksy admin interface.

The selection applies to the current Shopify staff account in the current browser. It does not translate storefront product content, the theme, or another staff member's Looksy interface.
