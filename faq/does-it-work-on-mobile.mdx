## Can shoppers use Looksy on mobile?

Looksy provides a browser-based shopper flow, but mobile behavior depends on the device, browser, permissions, theme, network, and input image. Test the complete experience on the devices that matter to your store.

## What should I test?

- current iOS and Android devices used by your shoppers;
- Safari and Chrome, plus other browsers that represent meaningful traffic;
- taking a photo and selecting an existing image;
- permission denied, upload failure, processing, and retry states;
- portrait and landscape layouts;
- product and variant selection after returning to the page;
- slower connections and older supported devices.

## Does a shopper need camera access?

Camera permission may be needed when a shopper chooses to take a new photo. An existing image may be selected through the browser's upload flow. Exact options can vary by device and browser.

## What if mobile does not work?

Record the product URL, device, operating-system version, browser version, theme, connection type, and exact steps. Confirm that permissions and the selected image are valid, then reproduce the issue before contacting support.

<Note>
  Do not assume identical performance or compatibility across every mobile device and browser. Verify with current store traffic and representative hardware.
</Note>
