## What does Looksy cost?

Looksy has a free Preview plan with 25 credits. Paid plans start at **$14.99 per month**. The current plans are:

| Plan | Monthly price | Included credits | Additional credit | Annual price |
| --- | ---: | ---: | ---: | ---: |
| Preview | $0 | 25 | Not listed | Not listed |
| Starter | $14.99 | 100 | $0.14 | $149 |
| Growth | $29 | 300 | $0.12 | $290 |
| Scale | $79 | 600 | $0.10 | $790 |

<Note>
  Pricing can change. Confirm the offer shown in Looksy before subscribing. The [Looksy website](https://withlooksy.com/) and [Shopify App Store listing](https://apps.shopify.com/looksy) are the public sources for current pricing.
</Note>

## Which plan should I choose?

- **Preview:** Use the included 25 credits to evaluate Looksy before choosing a paid plan.
- **Starter:** Choose this when 100 included credits match your expected usage.
- **Growth:** Choose this when you need 300 included credits and a lower additional-credit rate.
- **Scale:** Choose this when you need 600 included credits and the lowest listed additional-credit rate.

Compare your recent usage with each plan's included credits. If usage is uncertain, start with the smallest suitable plan and review the usage shown in Looksy.

## What are additional credits?

Additional credits are credits used beyond the amount included in a paid plan. The listed per-credit rate depends on the plan: $0.14 on Starter, $0.12 on Growth, and $0.10 on Scale.

For how credits relate to your usage, see [Understanding Credits](https://withlooksy.com/docs/getting-started/understanding-credits).

## Where can I verify billing details?

Check the pricing and subscription information displayed in Looksy before confirming a plan. For questions about payment methods, refunds, cancellation timing, taxes, or account-specific billing, use the support option in Looksy rather than relying on an older copy of this page.
