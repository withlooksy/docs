## What does Looksy cost?

Looksy has a free Preview plan with 25 credits. Paid plans start at **$14.99 per month**. The current in-app Plans screen starts monthly subscriptions:

| Plan | Monthly price | Included credits | Additional credit on monthly billing | Annual catalog price |
| --- | ---: | ---: | ---: | ---: |
| Preview | $0 | 25 | Not listed | Not listed |
| Starter | $14.99 | 100 | $0.14 | $149 |
| Growth | $29 | 300 | $0.12 | $290 |
| Scale | $79 | 600 | $0.10 | $790 |

<Note>
  The annual amounts are retained in Looksy's billing catalog for annual subscriptions and migration of existing annual subscriptions. They are not a self-service choice in the current in-app Plans screen. Confirm the exact cadence and charge shown in Shopify before approval; contact Looksy support if you need an annual option.
</Note>

## Which plan should I choose?

- **Preview:** Evaluate Looksy with up to 25 successful try-ons in a rolling 30-day window. Preview is not a Shopify subscription.
- **Starter:** Choose this when 100 included credits match your expected usage.
- **Growth:** Choose this when you need 300 included credits and a lower additional-credit rate.
- **Scale:** Choose this when you need 600 included credits and the lowest listed additional-credit rate.

Compare your recent usage with each plan's included credits. If usage is uncertain, start with the smallest suitable plan and review the usage shown in Looksy.

## What are additional credits?

Additional credits are successful try-ons beyond the amount included in a **monthly** paid plan. The listed per-credit rate is $0.14 on Starter, $0.12 on Growth, and $0.10 on Scale. The usage charge and its cap appear in Shopify's subscription approval flow.

Annual subscriptions do not carry Looksy's usage-overage line. Looksy evaluates their included allowance over a rolling 30-day usage window. When that allowance is used, new try-ons pause rather than creating per-credit overage.

<Warning>
  The additional-credit prices in the table apply to monthly billing. Do not use them to estimate annual-plan overage. If a public marketing page disagrees with the plan or Shopify approval shown in Looksy, stop and contact support before subscribing.
</Warning>

For how credits relate to your usage, see [Understanding Credits](https://withlooksy.com/docs/getting-started/understanding-credits).

## Why am I being asked to confirm my existing plan?

Looksy is moving existing subscriptions to Shopify's native billing. If the app shows **Action needed: confirm your plan**, your current plan continues to work, but it needs a one-time confirmation through Shopify before **September 30, 2026**.

Review the plan name, price, and credits shown in Looksy, then approve the charge through Shopify. The confirmation is for the charge you already pay; Looksy states that the migration keeps the current price and credits unchanged. If the shown details do not match your subscription, stop and contact support before approving anything.

## What happens if I cancel?

The current Looksy billing flow ends the subscription immediately and returns the store to Preview, subject to its 25-successful-try-ons rolling-30-day allowance and usage already recorded in that window. New try-ons pause when the remaining allowance is used.

Review the cancellation confirmation in Looksy before proceeding. For account-specific refunds, taxes, or billing corrections, contact support.

## What if Looksy cannot load or match my subscription?

- **Action needed: choose a plan:** Looksy could not match the current subscription to a standard catalog plan. The existing subscription remains active; Looksy does not replace it until you choose and approve a plan through Shopify.
- **Plans couldn't load / Billing unavailable:** retry from the page before making a plan decision. Do not infer that the store is on Preview while billing status is unavailable.
- **Reconnect Shopify:** restore the Shopify connection, then reload billing. Looksy cannot safely read or change a subscription without the required Shopify billing access.

If the status remains ambiguous, contact support with the store domain and the exact message shown. Do not approve a replacement plan until its name, cadence, price, and credits match what you expect.

## Where can I verify billing details?

Check the pricing and subscription information displayed in Looksy before confirming a plan. The [Shopify App Store listing](https://apps.shopify.com/looksy) is the public monthly-pricing reference. For annual availability, payment methods, refunds, cancellation timing, taxes, or account-specific billing, use the support option in Looksy rather than relying on a marketing page or an older copy of this documentation.
