## Understanding "Try-On Credits"

### What Counts as a Credit?

One credit = one customer uploading a photo and seeing a result.

**Examples:**
- Customer uploads 1 photo on 1 product = **1 credit**
- Customer uploads 1 photo on 3 different products = **3 credits**
- Same customer returns next day and tries on 2 products = **2 more credits**

### What If I Exceed My Limit?

**Option 1:** Purchase additional credits at your plan's rate:
- Starter: $0.12 per additional credit
- Growth: $0.10 per additional credit
- Scale: $0.08 per additional credit

**Option 2:** Upgrade to the next plan

**Option 3:** Contact support for custom enterprise pricing

<Tip>
Start with the free plan to test, then upgrade based on your actual usage patterns.
</Tip>

## How to Choose the Right Plan

**Best for:**
- Brand new stores just getting started
- Testing if virtual try-on works for your products
- Very small boutiques with minimal traffic
- Proof of concept before investing

### Starter Plan ($29/month)

**Best for:**
- Small stores (< 5,000 monthly visitors)
- New to virtual try-on
- Focused product lines
- Stores with < 200 orders/month
