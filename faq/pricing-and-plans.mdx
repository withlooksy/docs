## Understanding "Try-Ons"

### What Counts as a Try-On?

One try-on = one customer uploading a photo and seeing a result.

**Examples:**
- Customer uploads 1 photo on 1 product = **1 try-on**
- Customer uploads 1 photo on 3 different products = **3 try-ons**
- Same customer returns next day and tries on 2 products = **2 more try-ons**

### What If I Exceed My Limit?

**Option 1:** Upgrade to the next plan automatically

**Option 2:** Purchase overage packs ($25 for 500 additional try-ons)

**Option 3:** Contact support for custom enterprise pricing

<Tip>
Most stores use 20-30% of their plan's try-on limit. Start with a smaller plan and upgrade if needed.
</Tip>
