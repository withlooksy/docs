## Which products can I use with Looksy?

Product suitability must be verified with the actual item, imagery, variant data, and storefront template. A broad product category alone does not establish that every item will provide an acceptable result.

## What should I test?

- representative cuts, materials, colours, and construction;
- products with different image layouts;
- each important variant path;
- mobile and desktop storefront templates;
- accepted inputs, failures, and retries;
- whether the result helps the intended shopper decision.

## What can make a product harder to use?

Incomplete or inconsistent images, unusual construction, fine details, layering, complex accessories, variant mismatches, and customized product templates can affect the experience.

## What if a product does not meet my standard?

Record the product, variant, source image, device, browser, and exact steps. Exclude the product from rollout until the issue is understood or corrected.
