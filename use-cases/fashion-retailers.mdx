## How should a fashion retailer introduce Looksy?

Begin with a controlled product set, test the shopper journey, and expand based on evidence. Virtual try-on should complement accurate photography, fit information, variants, delivery details, and return policies.

## Select the first product group

Prefer products with:

- clear source images;
- enough traffic to evaluate;
- a meaningful visual decision for the shopper;
- representative variants;
- reliable product data.

## Test before expanding

1. Verify automatic product-image placement and mobile usability.
2. Complete the shopper flow for representative products and variants.
3. Review processing, failure, and retry states.
4. Define the measurement period and comparison group.
5. Expand, refine, or remove the experience based on store data.

## What should I measure?

Use try-on starts and completions as diagnostic signals. Evaluate business impact with add-to-cart rate, conversion, support questions, return reasons, and contribution margin.

<Note>
  Merchant outcomes vary. Looksy does not guarantee a specific conversion lift, return-rate reduction, or ROI.
</Note>
