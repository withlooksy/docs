## How can activewear stores use Looksy?

Looksy can give shoppers another way to visualize activewear before buying. Pair it with accurate information about fit, support, fabric, opacity, construction, and intended activity.

## Which products should I test first?

Choose products that have good source images, meaningful traffic, and a clear shopper question that visualization may help answer. Include representative colours, cuts, and variants.

## What should I verify?

- the **Try On** button is clear over the main product image;
- variants remain clear before and after the flow;
- mobile upload and processing states work as expected;
- output and product information set compatible expectations;
- failures explain the next action.

## How should I measure it?

Track usage and completion alongside add-to-cart rate, conversion, support questions, and return reasons. Compare equivalent products or use an A/B test where possible.

<Note>
  Looksy does not guarantee a conversion lift, return-rate reduction, or ROI for activewear products.
</Note>
