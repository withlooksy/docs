## Success Factors

### What Top Performers Do Differently

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="1. Multiple Product Images">
    **Top performers:**
    - Multiple angles (max 4 images per product)
    - Detail shots showing texture and material
    - Higher resolution helps (1200px+)
    - Works with any background

    **Impact:** 15-25% higher conversion on try-on
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="2. Button Customization">
    **Top performers:**
    - Clear button text ("Try On" or "See it on you")
    - High contrast colors via dashboard customization
    - Visible corner position (test top left vs top right)

    **Impact:** Testing button copy and colors can improve engagement
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="3. Active Promotion">
    **Top performers:**
    - Homepage banner
    - Email campaigns
    - Social media posts
    - In-app messaging

    **Impact:** 20-40% higher awareness and usage
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="4. Mobile Experience">
    **What works:**
    - Mobile works automatically (no optimization needed)
    - Native camera integration
    - Works on all devices and browsers

    **Impact:** 70%+ of try-ons happen on mobile automatically
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="5. Data-Driven Optimization">
    **Top performers:**
    - Monitor analytics weekly
    - Test button copy via dashboard
    - Focus on top products (via analytics)
    - Add multiple product angles and detail shots

    **Impact:** 15-30% improvement over time
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
