## Understanding Your Looksy Dashboard

The Looksy analytics dashboard shows you how virtual try-on is performing on your store.

## Accessing Your Dashboard

1. Go to **Apps** in your Shopify admin
2. Click **Looksy**
3. View your analytics

## Available Metrics

### Total Try-Ons

Total number of virtual try-ons completed on your store.

**What it shows:**
- Total try-ons over time
- Overall usage of the feature

**Why it matters:**
- Indicates feature adoption
- Shows customer engagement with virtual try-on

### Total Remaining Credits

Your current credit balance.

**What it shows:**
- Credits remaining in your account
- Usage rate

**Why it matters:**
- Helps you plan when to upgrade or add credits
- Tracks monthly usage

### Top Products

Products with the most virtual try-ons.

**What it shows:**
- Which products customers try on most
- Product ranking by try-on volume

**Why it matters:**
- Identifies products customers want to visualize
- Helps focus marketing on high-engagement items

### Amount of Try-Ons Per Product

Try-on count for each individual product.

**What it shows:**
- Try-on volume per product
- Product-level engagement

**Why it matters:**
- See which products benefit most from virtual try-on
- Identify products to promote or optimize

### Recent Activity

Recent virtual try-on activity on your store.

**What it shows:**
- Latest try-ons
- Real-time activity feed

**Why it matters:**
- See customer engagement in real-time
- Monitor feature usage

## How to Use Analytics

### Track Overall Usage

Monitor total try-ons to see how adoption grows over time.

**Good performance:**
- Steady growth in try-ons month-over-month
- Increasing usage as you promote the feature

### Identify Top Products

Look at top products to see what customers engage with most.

**Actions you can take:**
- Feature top try-on products in marketing
- Promote virtual try-on on product pages with highest usage
- Use product insights to guide inventory decisions

### Monitor Credits

Keep an eye on remaining credits to avoid running out.

**Best practices:**
- Check credits weekly
- Plan ahead for high-traffic periods
- Upgrade plan if consistently running low

### Watch Recent Activity

See real-time try-ons to understand when customers use the feature.

**What to observe:**
- Peak usage times
- Customer behavior patterns
- Feature adoption after marketing campaigns
