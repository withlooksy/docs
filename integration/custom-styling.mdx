## Customize the Button in Your Dashboard

Customize the Try On button to match your brand using the Looksy dashboard.

## Available Customizations

### Button Text

**Default:** "Try On"

**Alternatives:**
- "See it on you"
- "Virtual try-on"
- "Try it on"
- "Preview on yourself"
- Custom text of your choice

**Best practice:** Keep it short (2-4 words) and action-oriented.

### Button Colors

**What you can customize:**
- Background color
- Text color
- Button styling

**Best practice:** Choose colors that match your brand and contrast well for visibility.

### Corner Position

**Options:**
- Top left (default)
- Top right

**Best practice:** Choose based on your product image layout and other overlays.

## How to Customize

1. Go to your **Looksy dashboard**
2. Click **"Customize Button"**
3. Adjust the following:
   - Button text
   - Button colors (background, text)
   - Corner position (top left or top right)
4. Click **"Save"**

<Check>
Changes apply to all products automatically on both desktop and mobile.
</Check>

## Customization Limits

You can only customize:
- Button text
- Button colors
- Corner position

You cannot customize:
- Button location (fixed on product image)
- CSS styling beyond dashboard settings
- Modal appearance
- Upload area styling
- Fonts or typography
- Animations

<Note>
All customization is done via the Looksy dashboard. You cannot add custom CSS or code to further customize the button.
</Note>

## Brand Styling Examples

### Minimalist/Clean Brands
- Text: "Try On"
- Colors: Black button, white text
- Position: Top left

### Vibrant/Fun Brands
- Text: "See it on you!"
- Colors: Bright brand color, contrasting text
- Position: Top right

### Luxury/Premium Brands
- Text: "Preview"
- Colors: Dark neutral, gold/elegant accent
- Position: Top left

## Testing Your Customization

After customizing:

1. Visit a product page on your live store
2. Check that button appears with your styling
3. Click button to verify try-on works
4. Test on mobile to confirm it looks good

<Check>
Customization applies automatically to desktop, mobile, and tablet. No separate testing needed.
</Check>

## Common Questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Can I add custom CSS to style the button?">
    No. You can only customize via the Looksy dashboard settings (text, colors, corner position). Custom CSS is not supported.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I change the button position?">
    No. The button is fixed on the product image. You can only change the corner position (top left or top right).
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I customize the modal or upload area?">
    No. Only the button text, colors, and corner position can be customized.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Do changes apply to mobile automatically?">
    Yes. All customization applies to desktop, mobile, and tablet automatically.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I use different styling for different products?">
    No. Button customization applies to all products globally.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
