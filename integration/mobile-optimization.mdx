## How do I optimize Looksy for mobile shoppers?

Start with the actual mobile journey: product page, **Try On** button over the main product image, image selection, processing, preview in the same image area, variant selection, add to cart, and recovery from failure.

## Mobile QA matrix

Include representative combinations from your analytics:

- device and screen size;
- operating-system version;
- browser and version;
- Wi-Fi and slower mobile network;
- portrait and landscape orientation;
- camera capture and existing-image selection;
- accepted, denied, and previously denied permissions.

## Usability checks

- The button stays on the main product image without covering important details, gallery controls, or product controls.
- Instructions fit on screen and use plain language.
- Tap targets are large enough and not obscured.
- Progress is clear while processing.
- Errors explain what happened and what to do next.
- A shopper can return to the correct product and variant.
- The flow remains usable with zoom and larger text.

## Performance checks

Measure product-page loading before and after enabling Looksy, then measure the interactive try-on flow separately. Review LCP, INP, CLS, long tasks, transferred data, and visible waiting time on representative hardware.

## Privacy checks

Explain why a shopper is being asked for an image or permission before they continue. Review the current [Looksy privacy policy](https://withlooksy.com/legal/privacy-policy) and confirm account-specific questions with support.

## What should I monitor after launch?

Segment starts, completions, failures, add-to-cart behavior, and support reports by device and browser. Re-test after theme, app, or browser changes.
