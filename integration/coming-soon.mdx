**Availability:** Coming soon.

Looksy currently shows these noninteractive integration cards:

- **Mailchimp**
- **Meta (Facebook & Instagram)**
- **Google Analytics 4**
- **SMS (Attentive & Postscript)**

They are roadmap previews, not merchant connection flows. Do not enter credentials, build production automations against them, or claim that Looksy currently sends data to these services.

The separate [Gorgias integration](https://withlooksy.com/docs/integration/gorgias) is also marked Coming soon. [Klaviyo](https://withlooksy.com/docs/integration/klaviyo) and [Shopify Flow](https://withlooksy.com/docs/integration/shopify-flow) are the currently available integration paths documented here.

## What should I do?

- Use only an integration whose card opens a real configuration flow in your Looksy app.
- Treat a **Coming soon** card as informational even if its description names intended events or audiences.
- Recheck the card and this documentation before planning a launch around a future integration.
- Contact support to register interest or confirm whether a controlled beta exists for your store.
