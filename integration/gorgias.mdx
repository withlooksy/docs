## Current status

**Availability:** Coming soon.

The current Looksy app lists the Gorgias integration as **Coming soon**. Its integration card is informational and does not provide a merchant connection flow.

<Warning>
Do not follow earlier Gorgias connection, OAuth, ticket-card, or agent-action instructions. Those instructions were published before merchant availability was verified and have been withdrawn.
</Warning>

## What is planned?

The Gorgias integration is intended to help support teams handle shopper-requested virtual try-ons inside a customer-support conversation. Final merchant steps, supported ticket types, consent controls, credit behaviour, and availability will be documented only after the released integration is verified.

## What should I do now?

- Continue using Looksy's storefront product-page try-on for shopper self-service.
- Treat the Gorgias card in Looksy as **Coming soon** while it remains noninteractive.
- Contact [caleb@withlooksy.com](mailto:caleb@withlooksy.com) to register interest or ask about availability for your store.

Do not send OAuth credentials or customer image files by email.
