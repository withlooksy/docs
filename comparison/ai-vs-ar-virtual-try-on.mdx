## AI Virtual Models vs AR: The Clear Winner for Fashion

When shopping for virtual try-on technology, you'll encounter two approaches: **AI-powered virtual models** (what Looksy uses) and **AR real-time overlays**. For fashion e-commerce, AI virtual models deliver significantly better results.

This guide explains why AI-powered virtual try-on outperforms AR for online apparel stores, backed by data and real merchant results.

## What is AI Virtual Try-On?

**AI virtual try-on** uses artificial intelligence to generate realistic images of products on a customer's body.

**How it works:**
1. Customer uploads a photo (selfie)
2. AI analyzes body shape, pose, and lighting
3. Generative AI model renders the garment on their body
4. Customer sees realistic image in 15-20 seconds
5. Result looks like a professional styled photo

**Technology:** Uses GANs (Generative Adversarial Networks) and CNNs (Convolutional Neural Networks) to create photorealistic visualizations.

**Example:** Looksy, virtual fitting rooms, AI styling tools

<Check>
AI virtual try-on creates static, high-quality images that show exactly how products look on the customer's body.
</Check>

---

## What is AR Virtual Try-On?

**AR virtual try-on** uses augmented reality to overlay products on a live camera feed in real-time.

**How it works:**
1. Customer opens camera on device
2. AR tracks face/body in real-time
3. Product overlays on live video feed
4. Customer moves and sees product from different angles
5. Experience happens in real-time (no processing wait)

**Technology:** Uses device cameras, depth sensors, and real-time rendering.

**Example:** Snapchat filters, Instagram AR effects, some virtual makeup apps

## When to Use AR Instead of AI

**AR is better for:**

1. **Eyewear and glasses** (best AR use case)
2. **Face-mounted accessories** (hats, headbands)
3. **Makeup and cosmetics** (see in real-time)
4. **Jewelry** (rings, bracelets - limited success)

**Why:** These products need real-time positioning on your face/hands. AI static images don't work as well.

**AR is worse for:**
- All apparel and clothing
- Full-body fashion
- Anything below the shoulders

<Warning>
If you sell fashion or apparel, AR is the wrong choice. AI delivers 2-3x better results for clothing.
</Warning>
