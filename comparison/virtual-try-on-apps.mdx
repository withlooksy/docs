## How should I compare virtual try-on apps?

Test each shortlisted app on the same products, theme, and devices. Use current first-party sources for pricing and product claims rather than relying on an undated comparison page.

## Evaluation checklist

### Shopper experience

- Is the purpose of the feature clear?
- Does the flow work on your priority mobile and desktop browsers?
- Are upload, processing, failure, and retry states understandable?
- Does the output set realistic expectations?

### Shopify implementation

- Can you place and style the experience appropriately in your theme?
- Does it behave correctly across representative products and variants?
- Can your team disable or adjust it when needed?
- Is support available for theme-specific issues?

### Measurement

- Which events and reports are available?
- Can you distinguish try-on users from other shoppers?
- Can you run a controlled test or a defensible comparison?
- Does the vendor avoid guaranteeing outcomes without evidence?

### Privacy and operations

- What shopper data is processed, retained, or shared?
- Where are current privacy terms documented?
- Who can access results and account data?
- What happens when processing fails?

### Cost

- Compare subscription price, included usage, additional usage, and operational work.
- Confirm all prices in the vendor's current billing surface before deciding.
